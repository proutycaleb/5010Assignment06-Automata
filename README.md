## Variation
For my variation of the cellular automota, I messed with the probability of the rules of the Game of Life being followed, primarily the deletion rules
In my version, there is only a 20% chance of a cell dying even if it meets the criteria of over/under population.

This creates an intresting effect where large stable blocks form over time, and "compete" for terretory at their intersection.
I'm not sure if it leads to an eventually stable system, but it does reduce in how much it changes over time. 
The motion reminds me of fire spreading, or of nations claming territory in newly colonized land.
